The MK54 Torpedo

MIDN Hannah LaVigne

MN 2004

The basics

  • Started in 1990s
  • Introduce in 2004
  • Lightweight Hybrid Torpedo (LHT)
  • Raytheon

Built from

  • MK 48 & MK 50 Torpedoes
    • Advanced capability programs
  • MK 46 Torpedo
    • Warhead
    • Propulsion System

Target

  • Submarines

Locating

  • Active acoustic homing
  • Semi-active acoustic homing

Platforms

  • Helicopters
  • Fixed Winged Aircraft
  • Surface Ships

Explosive

  • MK 46 Torpedo’s warhead
  • ~97 pounds
  • Polymer bonded explosive
  • Chemical explosion

Damage

  • Doesn’t run into target
  • Detonates close to target
  • Creates steam bubble
  • Cyclic stress
